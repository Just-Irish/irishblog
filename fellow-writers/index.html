Fellow Writers & Gaming Blogs

Here are some excellent gaming blogs and writers I follow:

Friends of Mine

  • The Leanto - Brandon and Richard are quite thought provoking and I regularly look forward to reading what they have to share.
  • Unturned Hovel - Another (or the new) project of Brandon as The Leanto has fallen silent.

Important to the Gaming Sphere

  • Gaming on Linux - A news source by Liam Dawe who focuses on Linux gaming and all news associated with it.

Tabletop News and Blogs

  • Grognardia - A fellow blogger who focuses on tabletop.
  • Roleplaying Tips - A tabletop blogger focusing on giving you tips to make you a better Dungeon Master.
  • The Alexandrian - Full of a lot of useful information, news and updates by Justin Alexander, an Ennie Award-winning game designer and writer.

Tabletop Resources

  • DunGen - Need to generate a quick dungeon for your tabletop session? DunGen has you covered.
  • Dungeon Scrawl - Want to create your own quick dungeon? D-Scrawl is where it’s at.
  • PapersPencils - Want a dungeon made from rolling a d100? This works great, but there is nothing visual, it’s all imagination.
  • Kassoon - Need some random items generated whether they are magical or junk? Kassoon has some generators for many things for you to take advantage of.

Know a great gaming blog? Let me know!
